### Pumpkin Bread (9'' x 5'' loaf)
Ingredients
1. 2 cups flour
2. 1/2 tsp. salt
3. 1/2 tsp. baking powder
4. 1 tsp. baking soda
5. 2 tsp. cinnamon
6. 1 1/2 cups sugar
7. 1/2 cup butter, melted
8. 2 eggs
9. 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree

Steps
1. Mix tog. dry ingr.
2. Add wet ingr.
3. Pour into greased pan.
4. Bake @375 degrees F for 40-50 min., until toothpick comes out clean
